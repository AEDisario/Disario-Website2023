DISARIO was founded by Alessandro Disario with the aim of offering an excellent and lasting product. Reflecting the origins of the founder, who was born in Holland to Italian parents, the brand unites the high level of Italian manufacturing with a more clean northern aesthetic.

Alessandro has always been compelled by a really good product, be it a well-prepared meal or a perfectly cut shirt. During his years in Florence, where he went to study mathematics, this curiosity slowly materialized. Standing in a tradition of northerners who have been profoundly moved during their stay in Italy, he felt the wish to create something of value. Clothing and accessoires that enhance the act of dressing.

In selecting the materials for our clothes and accessories, we concentrate on the touch and feel of the fabric. As a result, we plan to only gradually add products to our collection.

Ultimately, we want the wearer to feel the care and consideration put into each piece.
