Soft Track Jacket
Technical Cotton

Made with a with a fluid and durable technical cotton. The soft track jacket is a more soft and elegant interpretation of the classical track jacket, also shown by its smooth two-way Raccagni zip. Equally beautiful worn with jeans or more formal trousers. The soft track jacket is designed with a looser fit, for comfort.

Made in Italy

- Relaxed cut
- True To size
- Composition: 54% PL - 46% CO

materials and origin

- The technical cotton of the soft track jacket is water repellent and has been woven in Como (Italy), by a company specialised in technical fabrics.

- The soft track jacket has a two-way Raccagni zip. Made in italy.

- The jacket has been crafted by a small family owned company in Tuscany, Italy
