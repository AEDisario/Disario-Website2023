Weekend Shirt
White Cotton Poplin

With our weekend shirt we offer all the standards of a sartorial shirt, but with a softer construction. Having quality in something that can be worn with ease. Crafted from a smooth italian poplin, the weekend shirt has been designed with a slightly looser fit. The weekend shirt can really be worn any day of the week.

Made in Italy

- Regular fit
- True to size
- 100% Cotton
- Sizes: 38-39-40-41-42-43-44

materials and origin

- The cotton used for the weekend shirt is a THOMAS MASON TWO-PLY POPLIN COTTON. THOMAS MASON FABRICS HAVE, BECAUSE OF THEIR EXCEPTIONAL QUALITY, FOR CENTURIES BEEN USED BY THE BRITISH ARISTOCRACY FOR THEIR BESPOKE SHIRTS. WITH OUR WEEKEND SHIRT WE WANT TO OFFER THE QUALITY OF A BESPOKE SHIRT WITH A SOFTER, MORE RELAXED CONSTRUCTION.

- The buttons are mother of pearl (IT).

- OUR WEEKEND SHIRT HAS BEEN EXPERTLY CRAFTED BY SKILLED SHIRTMAKERS IN NAPOLI, ITALY. The city famous in italy for its shirt making tradition.
