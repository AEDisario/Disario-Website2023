Square Buckle Belt
Tuscan Leather

Both in design and manufacturing our belt is intended to last many years. The vegetable tanned leather is carefully selected in Tuscany and with it's sleek look the belt is equally beautiful with both jeans and formal trousers.

Made in Italy

- Height 3cm
- Sizes: 80-85-90-95-100-105

materials and origin

- The belt is made with a full grain vacchetta leather and has been vegetably tanned, a coloring process in which no chemicals are used. The tannery where we got the leather for our belt is located in Santa Croce sull'Arno, an area in Tuscany with a rich tradition in processing leather.

- The buckle is made of solid brass and has been satinated with a nickle free polish. The buckle is made in italy.

- Our Belt has been crafted by skilled leather artisans in Tuscany, Italy. The company manufacturing our belts has been making belts for over 50 years.
